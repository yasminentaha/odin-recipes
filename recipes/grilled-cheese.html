Grilled Cheese

A grilled cheese is a pretty common sandwich, which became popular in the United States during the Great Depression. It is prepared by heating one or more slices of cheese between slices of bread, with a cooking fat such as butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 or 2 slices of American cheese, or cheese of your choice
  • 2 tbps of butter

Steps

  1. Heat a large grill pan over high heat.
  2. Butter each side of the bread, then lay half of the bread, butter side down, in the pan.
  3. Put a slice of American cheese on each piece of bread.
  4. Grilled until both sides are golden brown, or until the cheese has reached its peak melty-goodness!
